# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product summary
RealEstateCRM is a modular, AI-powered operating system for real estate teams and adjacent businesses. It unifies CRM, lead generation, deal tracking, communication, marketing, voice AI, and client experience into one synced platform for web and mobile.

## 2. Product vision
Build a premium, readable, role-based platform that feels like a complete business operating system instead of a basic contact manager.

## 3. Primary users
- Broker / Team Lead
- Agent
- Admin / Operations
- Client (Buyer / Seller / Renter / Prospect)
- Partner users (Mortgage, Title, Insurance, Vendor)

## 4. Core outcomes
1. Centralize leads, listings, tasks, messages, files, workflows, and marketing.
2. Let AI help qualify leads, answer questions, route work, and generate content.
3. Keep every workflow area extensible.
4. Sync user activity across web and mobile.
5. Support client-facing and partner-facing collaboration.

## 5. Scope

### In scope
- CRM core: contacts, leads, deals, tasks, notes, activities, tags, pipelines, custom fields
- Lead capture and import from forms, CRMs, and third-party providers
- AI lead qualification through phone, text, and email
- Listings and transaction tracking
- Document upload, requests, and e-signature workflow
- Communication hub with email, SMS, calls, private chat, group chat, and deal chat
- Social and messaging integrations with permissions
- Voice AI with speech-to-text and text-to-speech choices
- Learning and memory controls
- Marketing studio with finished outputs
- AI promotional video pipeline from property photos
- Client portal
- Partner modules
- Mobile and web parity

### Out of scope for initial MVP
- Custom MLS replacement
- Full accounting/ledger replacement
- Custom social network creation
- Native video editor parity with pro tools

## 7. UX requirements
- Luxury visual style
- High readability
- Simple navigation
- Easy access to settings and appearance controls
- Clear role-based views
- Mobile-first dependability for on-the-go work

## 8. Success metrics
- Faster lead response times
- Higher lead qualification accuracy
- Higher appointment set rate
- Lower missed follow-ups
- Better content production speed
- Fewer manual sync issues on mobile
- High user adoption across roles

## 9. Primary product principles
- Be modular
- Be extensible
- Be readable
- Be role-aware
- Be dependable on mobile
- Keep workflow creation visible and easy
- Make AI helpful but controllable
