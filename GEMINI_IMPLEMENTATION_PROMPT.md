# Gemini Build Prompt

You are building a premium, modular real estate CRM called RealEstateCRM for Excel Legacy Realty Team.

## Goal
Create a web + mobile CRM and operating system that combines the practical strengths of Lofty and MyRealSource, while extending them with AI lead qualification, omnichannel communication, client portals, social publishing, voice AI, learning/memory, marketing studio outputs, and an AI promotional video pipeline from property photos.

## Non-negotiables
- Luxury but readable UI: black / charcoal base, deep blue accents, gold highlights, accessible contrast
- Support light/dark and classic/modern appearance modes
- Role-based access for broker/team lead, agent, admin, client, and partner users
- Visible Add Workflow / Add Step controls in workflow areas
- Web and mobile parity
- Offline-friendly mobile behavior with queued sync and recovery
- Audit trail for sensitive actions
- Extensible architecture for future add-ons

## Must include
1. CRM core: contacts, leads, deals, listings, tasks, notes, activities, tags, pipelines, custom fields
2. Lead capture and import from existing CRMs and third-party providers
3. AI lead generation agent that can call, text, email, qualify, route, and create tasks
4. Number qualification and contact validation before outreach
5. Communication hub with email, SMS, call logging, private chat, group chat, deal chat, client portal chat
6. Social / channel management with permissions, publishing, unified inbox, and audit logs
7. Voice AI with selectable STT/TTS providers, push-to-talk, hands-free mode, and conversational flow
8. Learning & Memory center with session memory, preference memory, workspace memory, conversation history, and feedback controls
9. Marketing studio for finished outputs such as flyers, postcards, door hangers, business cards, social creatives, and videos
10. Promotional video pipeline from property photos to finished marketing videos
11. Client portal for buyers, sellers, renters, and prospects
12. Partner modules for mortgage, title, insurance, and other adjacent businesses
13. Deal tracker / transaction management
14. Documents and e-signature flows
15. Reporting and analytics

## Product direction
- Build as a modular system, not a monolith of hard-coded screens
- Keep navigation simple
- Make the AI helpful but controllable
- Preserve conversation and workflow state across devices
- Treat the CRM timeline as the source of truth for all actions

## Suggested first build order
1. App shell and auth
2. CRM core data model
3. Leads and deal pipeline
4. Communication hub
5. Voice AI and learning/memory
6. Social management
7. Marketing studio
8. Promotional video pipeline
9. Client portal
10. Partner modules
11. Polish and performance

## Output expectation
When building, produce working screens, reusable components, data models, and clear user flows. Prefer incremental delivery with visible progress.
