     1|# Gemini Build Prompt
     2|
     3|You are building a premium, modular real estate CRM called RealEstateCRM for Excel Legacy Realty Team.
     4|
     5|## Goal
     6|Create a web + mobile CRM and operating system that combines the practical strengths of Lofty and MyRealSource, while extending them with AI lead qualification, omnichannel communication, client portals, social publishing, voice AI, learning/memory, marketing studio outputs, and an AI promotional video pipeline from property photos.
     7|
     8|## Non-negotiables
     9|- Luxury but readable UI: black / charcoal base, deep blue accents, gold highlights, accessible contrast
    10|- Support light/dark and classic/modern appearance modes
    11|- Role-based access for broker/team lead, agent, admin, client, and partner users
    12|- Visible Add Workflow / Add Step controls in workflow areas
    13|- Web and mobile parity
    14|- Offline-friendly mobile behavior with queued sync and recovery
    15|- Audit trail for sensitive actions
    16|- Extensible architecture for future add-ons
    17|
    18|## Must include
    19|1. CRM core: contacts, leads, deals, listings, tasks, notes, activities, tags, pipelines, custom fields
    20|2. Lead capture and import from existing CRMs and third-party providers
    21|3. AI lead generation agent that can call, text, email, qualify, route, and create tasks
    22|4. Number qualification and contact validation before outreach
    23|5. Communication hub with email, SMS, call logging, private chat, group chat, deal chat, client portal chat
    24|6. Social / channel management with permissions, publishing, unified inbox, and audit logs
    25|7. Voice AI with selectable STT/TTS providers, push-to-talk, hands-free mode, and conversational flow
    26|8. Learning & Memory center with session memory, preference memory, workspace memory, conversation history, and feedback controls
    27|9. Marketing studio for finished outputs such as flyers, postcards, door hangers, business cards, social creatives, and videos
    28|10. Promotional video pipeline from property photos to finished marketing videos
    29|11. Client portal for buyers, sellers, renters, and prospects
    30|12. Partner modules for mortgage, title, insurance, and other adjacent businesses
    31|13. Deal tracker / transaction management
    32|14. Documents and e-signature flows
    33|15. Reporting and analytics
    34|
    35|## Product direction
    36|- Build as a modular system, not a monolith of hard-coded screens
    37|- Keep navigation simple
    38|- Make the AI helpful but controllable
    39|- Preserve conversation and workflow state across devices
    40|- Treat the CRM timeline as the source of truth for all actions
    41|

    46|4. Communication hub
    47|5. Voice AI and learning/memory
    48|6. Social management

    54|

    56|When building, produce working screens, reusable components, data models, and clear user flows. Prefer incremental delivery with visible progress.
